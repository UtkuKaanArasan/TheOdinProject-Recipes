Hamburger recipe

Note: This recipe is shortened and oversimplified

Ingredients:

  • 4 hamburger bread
  • 1 teaspoon butter
  • 300gr meat
  • 4 leaves lettuce

Method:

  1. Use your hands to form 4 hamburger patties 3/4 inch thick.
  2. Use your thumbs to make an indention in the middle of the patties.
  3. Grill over medium-high heat for about 4 minutes on each side.
