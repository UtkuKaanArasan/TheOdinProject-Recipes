Ingredients:

  • 6-8 large potatoes
  • 1 cup pickled gherkins (finely chopped))
  • 1 cup of grain corn (Canned)
  • 1 bowl of green-black olives (Seedless)

Method:

  1. After washing and drying the potatoes, first wrap them in baking paper, then aluminum foil and place them on the baking tray.
  2. We bake in an oven at 220 degrees for about 1.5 hours.
  3. Then we take it out of the oven and separate it from the papers and cook it uncovered for about half an hour. It may vary depending on the potato, check it and bake it again.
